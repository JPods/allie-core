principle: vehicle_path_for is for animation (stitches through stations). base_vehicle_path_for is for geometry (the guideway's own points). Any check that measures a specific guideway's own geometry must use base_vehicle_path_for. vehicle_path_for includes neighboring geometry and will produce false readings for any per-guideway spatial analysis.
