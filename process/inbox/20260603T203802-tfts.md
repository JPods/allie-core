  Every guideway segment has a physical direction: the pod enters at start_point and exits at end_point.
  Each gw_* track entity has a short edge (~222mm) placed at the entity's local origin (0,0,0),

  CP component, not a dead_cap_end entity (those have been removed from the system).

  ene_railroad controls[0]→controls[1] direction. If anti-parallel (dot product < 0), sp and ep are
  swapped and pts_mm is written in the corrected order.
