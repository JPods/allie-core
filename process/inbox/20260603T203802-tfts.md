  - gw_cp_in_lead_N: pod travels FROM the external stub (inbound from inter-station connector)

  - gw_cp_out_lead_N: pod travels FROM the interior junction (uturn or near_main)
    TOWARD the external stub (outbound to inter-station connector). start_point = junction end, end_point = stub end.

  The 0.222m vector at the outside bottom entry is the authoritative datum for which end is the external
  (stub/CP) end. If pts_mm in lines.json is populated but the direction is reversed, the animator
  interpolates the pod traversing backward — it appears to double back and the uturn occurs at the wrong end.
