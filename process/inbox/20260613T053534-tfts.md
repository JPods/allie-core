# TFTS — 2026-06-13T05:35:34Z

problem:   confirm_slots clears a freshly reserved slot while the pod is traveling to it
fault_ref: (observed in Shuffle Test log: "confirm_slots reconciled: ps7: NORA_0003→nil")

arc:
  - try:      confirm_slots rebuilds parking_slots entirely from SketchUp entity attribute
              scan (parked_station_id + parking_slot).  reserve_slot writes the slot to
              @@stations[:parking_slots] in memory; vehicle_parked writes entity attributes.
    result:   failed — a pod that reserve_slot placed at ps7 (status=0, in-transit) has no
              entity attributes yet.  Entity scan misses it; confirmed hash is built without
              it; parking_slots rebuilt from confirmed clears ps7 reservation.
              "confirm_slots reconciled: ps7: NORA_0003→nil" fires on next Natalie clock sweep.
    revealed: Two sources of truth for slot occupancy: (a) SketchUp entity attributes, written
              at park-complete; (b) in-memory @@stations[:parking_slots], written at reserve_slot.
              confirm_slots only read (a) and overwrote (b).  The window between reserve_slot
              and vehicle_parked is exactly when a second pod could steal the slot.

  - try:      Pass 2a in confirm_slots: after the entity scan, iterate @@stations[:parking_slots].
              Any pod registered there with vehicle status=0 (in-transit) is preserved in
              confirmed even if entity scan missed it.  Only moving pods are protected — parked
              pods (status=slot_n) must appear in the entity scan or they are stale and
              should be cleared.
    result:   succeeded — in-transit reservations survive the reconciliation.  No false
              "reconciled: psN→nil" log lines.  Slot collision window eliminated.

principle: confirm_slots must have two sources: (1) entity attributes (ground truth for
           physically-parked pods), and (2) in-memory vehicle status=0 entries (ground truth
           for in-transit reserved slots).  Rebuilding parking_slots from entity scan alone
           creates a slot-theft window between reserve_slot and vehicle_parked.
           Rule: status=0 + parking_slots entry = protected reservation; do not clear it.
