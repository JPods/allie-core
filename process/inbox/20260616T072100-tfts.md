principle: |
  BFS trip routing must stop ONE BEFORE the actual parking track (from Sally.parking_chain_for),
  not one before gw_platform_parking. gw_platform_parking is an approach siding for thru_dip —
  pods pass THROUGH it on the way to gw_platform (the real parking zone).

  The old at_parking condition stopped two hops early (at gw_platform_in), causing Trip 2 to
  seed the park_man from the wrong position (cross-track), producing CW backward motion.

  Rule: parking_chain in lines.json = parking track. Stop one before that. Never infer parking
  zone from track names in lookup_cache — presence of gw_platform_parking does not mean it is
  a parking zone. It is an approach siding unless parking_chain explicitly includes it.

  CCW invariant: pods only ever move forward (CCW) in a station. Trip 1 delivers to the track
  one before the parking zone. Trip 2 drives CCW from there to the assigned slot. advance_pod_slot
  only advances from lower to higher slot numbers (CCW along gw_platform).
