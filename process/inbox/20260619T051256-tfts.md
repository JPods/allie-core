principle: The animation timer's `dt` accumulates wall-clock time since the previous tick STARTED,
           not since the new maneuver was dispatched. For maneuvers dispatched during a slow
           tick (Sally dispatch work, model operations), the first tick's dt is inflated by the
           entire slow-tick duration. For short segments (< 5000mm), this can cause one-tick
           completion with a compressed actual_s measurement → false speed_deviation.
           Guard: on the first tick after dispatch (seg_start_tick == tick_count), cap dt to
           ANIM_INTERVAL. This is safe because normal-timing ticks already have dt ≈ ANIM_INTERVAL.

applies_to: jpod_vehicle_anim.rb NoraPod#propose — any short park/advance maneuver
            dispatched from a slow arrived_ids tick (Sally, model ops, lots of puts).
