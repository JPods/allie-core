Opportunity

Three forces are converging:

  • Big money flowing into podcar networks. Glydways locked $170M, chasing $250M more. Deployments in Atlanta, Miami, Abu Dhabi, Dubai, Tokyo, Hyderabad. Bill Gates: "AVs will change transportation as dramatically as the PC changed office work."
  • Supply chains ready. Robotics, solar, batteries are mass-produced commodities. The supply chain built for EVs is available to meet JPods demand at scale.
  • Recognition that urban highways harm prosperity hit a tipping point. 1.2% of US land is walkable yet produces 20% of GDP. Arlington, VA: 10% walkable land produces 50% of GDP. WalkScore over 70 areas collect enough tax revenue (sales + property) to maintain infrastructure — car-dependent sprawl does not. Donald Shoup (The High Cost of Free Parking), Jane Jacobs (Death and Life of Great American Cities), StrongTowns, and Urban3's fiscal productivity mapping all document the same finding: car infrastructure destroys the tax base it depends on. JPods converts low-tax parking lots to high-tax productive land. D+ road maintenance grades. Scooters and Uber prove the market is ready to shift.
What JPods Is

Solar-powered networks of self-driving vehicles on grade-separated guideways. The Physical Internet® (registered trademark). Horizontal Elevators™. Packet-switched transit — the same paradigm shift that let the Internet replace the telephone network.

  • 10X Energy Security: Eliminates oil-war risk. Solar self-reliant.
  • 10X less Congestion: Grade-separated. Zero traffic conflicts.
  • 10X less Pollution: Zero emissions. Zero CO2 production.
  • 10X greater Safety: 0.9 injuries/million (ASTM) vs 11,200 (DOT).
  • 10X less real estate: Guideways add a new dimension to urban land.
  • 10X less capital cost: $15M/two-way mile vs $70-150M for rail.
  • 10X cost savings: $0.03/passenger-mile vs $0.38 cars, $0.76 trains.
JPods vs. Glydways
  • Grade separation: JPods fully above traffic. Glydways at-grade.
  • Energy: JPods solar self-reliant. Glydways grid-dependent.
  • Funding: JPods privately funded (5X5 Standard). Glydways gov subsidies.
  • Patent: JPods US 6,810,817 (2002). Glydways: none on core concept.
  • Safety: JPods ASTM F24 (accountability) + ATN. Glydways ATN only (sovereign immunity).
  • Distribution: JPods bottom-up (free software + student kits). Glydways top-down (gov contracts).

Regulatory Framework — 5X5 Standard
  • Privately funded construction. No government subsidies.
  • Exceed 5× the efficiency of existing roads (125 mpg equivalent).
  • Pay 5% of gross revenues to rights-of-way holders.
  • Exceed safety of modes already approved (ASTM F24/F2291).
  • Collect 2+ MWh renewable energy per network mile per day.
  • Mike Teske: Certified 110 major rides for Disney and Universal Studios.
