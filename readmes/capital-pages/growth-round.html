The Scale Precedent

The Pacific Railroad Act of 1864 broke the deployment barrier. Four years later, 1,911 miles crossed the nation. 260,000 miles were built in 30 years. By 1916, every US city over 10,000 had a privately funded streetcar network.

Once the barrier breaks, deployment is explosive. Railroads proved it. The Internet proved it. Massachusetts broke the barrier for JPods in 2021. Capital accelerates what is already moving.
