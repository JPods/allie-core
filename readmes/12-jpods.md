## The 5X5 Standard

The mechanism for restoring transportation to free markets. A privately-funded mobility network receives rights-of-way if it meets five conditions:

1. **Privately funded** — no government construction money
2. **No subsidies** — operates on its own revenue
3. **5x efficiency** of existing roads (125 mpg equivalent or better)
4. **5% of gross revenues** to rights-of-way holders
5. **Exceeds safety** of modes already approved for those rights-of-way

The 5X5 Standard does for transportation what the 1982 AT&T breakup did for communications.

**Cities that have acted**: Macon GA (full franchise), North Central Texas Coalition (DFW 16 counties voted to allow), Atoka OK (MOU signed), Shaxian China (full franchise, awaiting US manufacturing).
