# Want a Sustainable Future? Create It.

**40,000 Americans die on roads every year.** That's a 9/11 every 27 days. The government's solution: wider highways that induce more traffic.

**We invented solar-powered transport networks in 2002.** Patent 6,810,817 — distributed networks of self-driving vehicles powered by solar energy integrated into the guideway structure. No human driver. No grid dependency. No government subsidy.

**In 2015, a New Jersey bureaucrat killed it with two sentences.** The Secaucus Town Council had unanimously approved our network. An unelected Deputy Commissioner wrote: "no practical application." The same technology class that delivered 100 million injury-free passenger-miles at Morgantown, WV since 1975.

**Massachusetts broke through.** In 2021, the Legislature unanimously funded $1.3 million for solar-powered mobility networks and the Governor signed it. The regulatory barrier that blocked innovation for a century — broken. Not by asking permission. By building proof until the evidence was undeniable.

**Now the world is catching up.** Glydways raised $170M+ and is deploying pod transit in Atlanta, Miami, Abu Dhabi, Dubai, Tokyo, and Hyderabad. Bill Gates: "AVs will change transportation as dramatically as the PC changed office work." The market JPods created 22 years ago is here.

**JPods has better physics.** Glydways runs at ground level on government money. JPods runs above traffic on solar power and private capital. Packet size drives network value — V ∝ n²/p. The smaller the vehicle, the more origin-destination pairs served, the higher the network value. JPods drives packet size toward one.
