## Lessons for Allie

1. **Remote branch is the reference** — When local commits accumulate Z fixes that all fail,
   and Bill points to the remote, the correct architecture is already there. At the start of
   any Z_CONTINUITY-class problem: check origin/su_jpods_claude first.

2. **Removed architecture stays removed** — When a concept (stubs, stub_tip_world) has been
   deliberately removed from the codebase, do not reintroduce it even if it looks like the
   shortest fix. Ask why it was removed.

3. **Synthesis beats reading** — When both gw_cp and seg_ Z are derived from cp_center.z,
   they match trivially. Find the canonical datum and derive both sides from it.
