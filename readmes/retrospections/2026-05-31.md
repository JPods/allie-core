## Lessons for Allie

1. **CP-unit grouping is the CCW ordering rule** — Not a topological traversal. All tracks
   associated with one CP listed as a unit: out_lead, uturn, out_stub, in_stub, in_lead.
   Then interior tracks. Then CP1 unit. Sally's BFS output does not match this. Fix
   jpod_sally.rb before the next round of template authoring.
   Domain: [SketchUp]

2. **Removing a track cascades through eps[]** — When a track doesn't exist in the model,
   two EPs collapse into one. All subsequent EP ids shift. Landing/exit chain switch records
   reference EP ids — they must be updated too. Always write the full corrected eps[] array;
   don't patch in place.
   Domain: [SketchUp]

3. **alpha pairs carry CCW position, not alpha position** — `[ccw_pos, "track"]` sorted
   alphabetically. Readers find a track's CCW position without scanning CCW array.
   Domain: [SketchUp]

4. **chains_header.approved_by = "" blocks Noelle Build** — Intentional gate. Don't set it
   until Bill has reviewed the chain paths. All 6 templates currently have approved_by empty.
   Domain: [SketchUp]
