### Z-bump investigation — root cause identified, fix deferred
Bill observed two Z anomalies in animation:
1. **seg_ tracks 312mm above gw_ tracks** — at every station-to-station junction
2. **gw_cp_in_lead still has 187.5mm internal bump** (extracted.json path only)

**Root cause of 187.5mm gw_cp_in_lead bump (extracted.json path):**
- gw_cp_in components in the template model are placed 187.5mm below ring_z (outside the formation component, at lower physical Z in the .skp file).
- Step 6 in the extracted.json path runs BEFORE `snap_to_cp_centers`. It snaps gw_cp_in_lead START down to gw_cp_in END — propagating the defective Z into gw_cp_in_lead.
- `snap_to_cp_centers` then moves gw_cp_in START to seg_Z, but gw_cp_in_lead is already poisoned AND is ~2500mm away in XY from any seg_ anchor (beyond the 600mm tolerance).
- **With geometry.json path**: gw_cp_in is already at ring_z (corrected in geometry.json), Step 6 doesn't run, gw_cp_in_lead stays at ring_z. The 187.5mm internal gw_ bump should disappear after plugin reload.

**Root cause of 312mm seg_→gw_ gap:**
- `beam_path` (seg_ tracks) stores beam-TOP centerline Z.
- geometry.json ring_z after world_xf is at a different vertical reference (approximately beam-CENTER).
- The gap = BEAM_DEPTH/2 (250mm) + rounding differences ≈ 312mm.
- Bill deferred fix: "live with it for now, add a fudge-factor."

**Planned fudge-factor approach:**
After `snap_to_cp_centers`, compute Z delta from adjacent seg_ endpoints (matched by station ID in CID). Apply uniform Z shift to all gw_ tracks for that station. Gate: only apply if delta is in range 50–1000mm (prevents accidental application to correctly-aligned stations).

### Sally backtrack fix — reserve_slot
Bill observed: "parking at s004 backtracked to parking space 1."

**Root cause:** Pod enters gw_platform at the slot-1 end (entry end, t=0) and advances toward slot N. When all slots 2..N are occupied, `downto(1)` returns slot 1. The animator drives the pod to t=0 — which is behind where the pod already was, causing a visible reversal.

**Fix:** In `reserve_slot`, if `slot == 1` AND `capacity > 1` AND higher slots are occupied → return nil. Pod is queued in gw_platform_parking and waits for a higher slot to free. Slot 1 is still assigned when it's the only slot (capacity=1) or when the pod is the first arrival (no occupied slots above it).

Committed: `jpod_sally.rb` — "Sally: refuse slot 1 when higher slots occupied — prevent backtrack"

## Lessons for Allie

1. **Step 6 (intra-station successor snap) poisons gw_cp_in_lead before snap_to_cp_centers runs** — Step 6 is inside the extracted.json else branch. It propagates a defective gw_cp_in Z into gw_cp_in_lead BEFORE the global snap pass. geometry.json path skips Step 6 entirely, so gw_cp_in_lead inherits the pre-corrected ring_z. When diagnosing "why did snap_to_cp_centers not fix X?", check whether Step 6 ran first and moved X beyond the 600mm snap radius.
   Domain tag: [SketchUp]

2. **Sally slot assignment: entry-end slot causes backtrack when pod has already advanced** — The pod enters gw_platform at t=0 (slot 1 end). Assigning slot 1 when higher slots are occupied means the animator reverses the pod. The fix is not in the animator — it's in Sally's reservation logic. Sally should refuse the entry-end slot when the pod has already advanced past it. This generalizes: any slot at t < pod's current t requires backtracking; Sally should only assign slots at t ≥ current position. For now, slot 1 is the only case because Sally already uses highest-first (`downto`).
   Domain tag: [SketchUp]

3. **Z reference mismatch: beam-TOP (seg_) vs. beam-CENTER (gw_) — 312mm systematic gap** — seg_ tracks come from `beam_path` which stores beam-TOP centerline. geometry.json ring_z after world_xf is at beam-CENTER. The gap ≈ BEAM_DEPTH/2 (250mm) + small offsets. This is structural, not random. The fudge-factor approach can mask it; the correct fix requires either: (a) extracting gw_ geometry at beam-TOP Z, or (b) bumping gw_ Z by +BEAM_DEPTH/2 during geometry.json generation. Document which reference each data store uses — Axiom 18.
   Domain tag: [SketchUp]
