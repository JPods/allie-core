## What to Watch For

**If % Accurate stays above 70%:** Real predictive knowledge in that domain.
Trust Claude/Allie judgment there more.

**If % Accurate is 40–70%:** Informed uncertainty. Use as a flag, not a conclusion.

**If % Accurate is below 40%:** Pattern-matching on noise. Either the questions
are too hard, the domain knowledge is insufficient, or the items aren't specific
enough to score. Adjust.

**If % Worthwhile is high but % Accurate is low:** The questions are good but
the predictions are overconfident. Ask the question; don't trust the answer.

**If % Accurate is high but % Worthwhile is low:** Predicting things that don't
matter. Shift to harder or more consequential questions.

**The ideal:** Items that are Worthwhile whether accurate or not — questions that,
just by being asked, force a check that would not have happened otherwise.
