Never frame JPods as anti-government. Bill's position is precise: the just purpose of government is to minimize violence from war and crime by coercing compliance with law. Innovation is a compliance failure. We need both government coercion to enforce safety AND liberty to allow Disruptive Minorities to innovate the general welfare. Each in their proper role.

**Why:** "Government produces more of what is failing" is a critique of government exceeding its proper role (mercantile monopolies, sovereign immunity), not a rejection of government itself. Bill is a West Point graduate and infantry veteran — he served the government. The argument is constitutional, not libertarian.

**How to apply:** When discussing regulation, frame as "proper roles" not "government vs freedom." ASTM F24 is where government coercion (insurance, enforcement, tort law) and liberty (no federal permission to innovate) work together. ATN sovereign immunity is government shielding itself from accountability — exceeding its proper role. The 5X5 Standard restores proper roles: government enforces safety, markets sort innovations.
