- 30% to walking/biking (Last-Mile: <3 mile trips)
- 52% of all US trips are under 3 miles
- Pricing: $1.25/km ($2.01/mi) — between Uber and car cost
- JPods actual cost: $7.4M/mile (from 5YearPLBS spreadsheet), NOT $15M
- Greenville: 54,646 trips/day, $264.6M/year, $554M capital = 2.1 year payback
- With cargo: 1.4 years
- Stress test: readmes/49-60pct-mode-shift-stress-test.md (18 objections ranked)
