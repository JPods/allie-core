## Patent Portfolio Strategy

**Capital pitch framing:** JPods will become iconic. But worst case — once mind-share becomes market-share, acquirers will pay for the IP. The patents provide a valuation floor.

**Three patents that anchor valuation:**
- **3-Tier Cargo** — freight is the undervalued half; streaming goods/waste changes city logistics
- **Distributed Data Clusters** — datacenters along guideway corridors; cooling, power, physical security
- **Energy Self-Reliant AGT** — solar/wind/kinetic microgrids integrated into structures; ultracapacitors, flywheels

These three cover the infrastructure plays that attract acquisition interest from logistics, tech, and energy companies.

## Foundational Patent 6,810,817 (expired — marketing value)

Life expired, but establishes priority and marketing claim:
- **"We invented solar-powered transport networks"**
- Robots on grade-separated guideways: "A method of controlling a transportation System for moving people, freight, and any combination whereof using a distributed network of intelligent devices without requiring the aid of a human driver"
- Networks are solar-powered: "The method… providing… Solar and wind power generators integrated into the physical Structure of Said transportation System…."
- No one else can claim prior art on this combination

## DirtPods — Unregulated Market Entry

DirtPods (off-road/construction/mining corridors) bypass urban transit regulation entirely. No DOT approval needed for private property, mining operations, construction sites, or agricultural corridors. This is the "build it where permission isn't required, prove it works, then expand" strategy.

**How to apply:** Lead capital conversations with the iconic vision. Back it with the IP floor. DirtPods answers "how do you get past regulation?" — you don't fight it, you go where it doesn't apply.
