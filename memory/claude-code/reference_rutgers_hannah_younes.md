**Dr. Hannah Younes, PhD**
Senior Research Specialist
Voorhees Transportation Center
Edward J. Bloustein School of Planning and Public Policy, Rutgers University

Created the crash database catalog (Crash_Database_2024.csv) that maps every US state's crash data sources — the foundation for JPods' all-severity crash overlay system.

**Research focus:** Sustainable and safe transportation, crash and medical records, built environment, geometric roadway design, active travel. Both qualitative (focus groups, surveys, interviews with state DOTs, transit agencies, municipal engineers) and quantitative (statistical regression).

**Background:** PhD 2021, B.S. Environmental Science and Policy 2015, University of Maryland College Park. Previously at Maryland Transportation Institute (MTI). U.S. DOT Outstanding Student of the Year 2020. Ann Wylie Dissertation Fellowship 2021.
