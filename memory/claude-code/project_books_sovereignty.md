Bill intends to write books on the constitutional framework connecting sovereignty, usufruct, mercantile monopoly, and the Liberty mechanism. Accumulate material from conversations.

**Core thesis threads (as of 2026-07-07):**

1. **Mercantile monopoly defined**: government coercion extended beyond violence into commerce
   - East India Company (trade)
   - Slavery / Dred Scott / Fugitive Slave Act (labor markets)
   - Federal highways / 1956 Federal-Aid Highway Act (transportation)
   - Same structure, same damage, same remedy

2. **The Liberty mechanism**: two tolerances intertwine in a Darwinian crucible
   - Tolerance of Disruptive Minorities offering choices
   - Tolerance of the Wisdom of the Many sorting choices in usufruct free markets
   - Innovation IS compliance failure — government must be constrained to respect it

3. **Usufruct free market** (not just "free market"):
   - Pirates operate in a predatory free market
   - Usufruct = use for profit without harm, return in better condition for Posterity
   - Government's police power defends against predation of the commons — its one legitimate market role
   - Three states: mercantile monopoly / predatory free market / usufruct free market

4. **Constitutional evidence chain**:
   - Sept 14, 1787: Convention voted 8-3 to forbid federal highways/canals (postroads.com/post-roads/)
   - 21 Presidential veto messages reinforcing restriction (postroads.com/vetoes/)
   - Confederate Constitution moved Post Roads + No Preference into Commerce Clause (postroads.com/confederate-constitution/)
   - Henry Clay's persistent efforts to federally tax for highways prompted the fix

5. **Oil depletes, ingenuity compounds**: the 10x paradigm shift
   - Oil infrastructure depreciates from day one
   - JPods/ingenuity infrastructure appreciates — signal loop gets smarter
   - $100-400/gal fully burdened fuel cost in Afghanistan (BG Steve Anderson)
   - 0-3 war record since West Point '72 — failure to build inclusive institutions

6. **Sovereignty = operational self-sufficiency**:
   - Locally governed, solar powered, built by local labor, maintained within local resources
   - That's an inclusive institution
   - Every JPods ecosystem project passes this test
   - What the US built in 3 lost wars fails this test

7. **1916 streetcar precedent**: every US city >10,000 had privately funded streetcar network
   - 260,000 miles, 470 ton-mpg railroads, Mobility As A Service
   - Federal central planners gave 90 cents on the dollar to destroy walkable cities
   - JPods restores what was destroyed, solar-powered, with digital design tools

8. **Mindshare creates marketshare when effectively executed**
   - Podcar race coming (JPods, Glydways, SwyftCities)
   - Open-source tools = mind-share = head start
   - Tools empower everyone to design networks in their community
